[edit] How to get started

  • Tips By Leon

1. Download OpenHoldem, OpenScrape and ManualMode off the Google source site. [1]
2. Find Table map for your casino site. The forum section which has alot of maps and discussion on this is here [2]and also found in this wiki here[3]
3. Get OpenHoldem connected and showing your cards on a site of your choice.
4. Now you need to program the logic. There is a few threads on the OpenHoldem School section on the forums. Right now the 2 most popular are JunkBot [4] and Winngy [5] Both of those forumlas should give you a good basis on how to write the code to create your own.
5. Next would be to register for a forum account and start searching for any of the answers you have along with asking questions that you cant find an answer to.
