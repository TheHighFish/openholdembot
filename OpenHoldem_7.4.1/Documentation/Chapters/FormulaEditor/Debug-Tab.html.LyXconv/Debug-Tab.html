Debug-Tab

A special “function” in the formula-editor is the debug-tab. It supports the evaluation of symbols, functions and mathematical expressions while being connected to a table, be it ManualMode, replay-frames or a life table. Using it is quite simple: one expression per line, each one after an equality-sign.
After you have entered the expressions you are interested in just click Calc or Auto and OpenHoldem will show you the results of all evaluations like in the picture below. The debug-tab is one of the most useful feature for developing and debugging your OpenHoldem formula set.
